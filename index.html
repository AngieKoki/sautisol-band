SAUTI SOL

ABOUT THE BAND

Sauti sol are a Kenyan afro pop band formed in Nairobi, Kenya by vocalist Bien-Aime Baraza, Willis Chimano and Savara Mudigi in 2005. Initially an a capella group, guitarist Polycarp Otieno joined before they named themselves Sauti

Band Members

  • Bien-Aimé Baraza – Songwriter, Guitar, Piano
  • Willis Chimano – Baritone, Performer, Keytar
  • Savara Mudigi – Vocalist, Producer, drums, Bass Guitar
  • Savara Mudigi – Vocalist, Producer, drums, Bass Guitar
